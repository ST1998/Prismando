It's an app for voice chat, video calls and text. It was born mainly to extend its passion for video games, but even more.

Download Discord on:
| Android | iPhone OS | Win, Mac, Linux |

If you want to try Discord, but you don't want / can register to be able to use it, you can do it directly on this page, by pressing the icon at the bottom right. Press Join to join in the conversation (if you haven't done it before) and you won't lose anything every time you reload the page. The limitations, however, are the following:

* Other rooms of Prismando Discordiana are not available in this version;
* It's not possible to do voice chat, only the text one;
* Nothing can be attached, not even the images.
