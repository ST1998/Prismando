( 1 ) What is the group about?

Prismando and derivatives is rooted in the discussion of arguments from various sectors (eg. journalism), it brings together all types of people to discuss with others, where it demands respect and understanding first and foremost. And so it has been since 2016, when it was in another form and still immature, improving over time. Each Telegram group is an ONLUS, carried out with passion without profit.

( 2 ) What does SFW and NSFW mean?

SFW (Safe for Work) means safe and/or inappropriate material will be censored. NSFW (Not SFW), means sexually explicit, vulgar or offensive material, embarrassing the user . Similar to VM18.

( 3 ) Do you collaborate with other groups?

We do not collaborate often, being an independent group, but if we have any requests, we first notice the aspects that interest us most.

( 4 ) As a group, is it peaceful?

We don't like to talk about other groups, we try to protect our users, protect them from malicious people, ensure that users are well and respected.

( 5 ) Why isn't there on WhatsApp?

WhatsApp has many limitations compared to Telegram and clearly shows the mobile phone number as user name.

( 6 ) Why the name Prismando?

The prism gives the idea of connection, which is also synonymous with community, with a lot of -ing as a present derivative suffix.

( 7 ) Average age?

The average age is varied, Prismando is suitable for all ages.

( 8 ) Why are some users offline?

For several reasons:

* Someone might lose interest after being on Prismando;

* They don't use Telegram for a long time;

* They are submerged by many groups or channels, therefore they no longer find Prismando.

( 9 ) Do you accept criticism?

Yes, they are useful to better understand user feedback, but be constructive, not to denigrate.
